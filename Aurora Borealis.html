The Northern Lights

'The Northern Lights' or the aurora borealis, are beautiful dancing waves of light that have captivated people for millennia. But for all its beauty, this spectacular light show ia a rather violent event. They occur so high up in the atmosphere that thay don't pose any threat to people watching them from the ground.
