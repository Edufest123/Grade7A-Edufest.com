WildFire

Wildfire is a very deadly phenomenon which can cause extinction of animal species, become causes of nearby people having lethal health problems and dieing and lot of emission of deadly gases in the surrounding. But we need not worry about Wildfires as we have Fire-Fixer , which is a machine that immediately detects the start of a wildfire and then releases a mixture of Carbon dioxide and a fire retardant that extinguish the Wildfire and stop it from spreading.
